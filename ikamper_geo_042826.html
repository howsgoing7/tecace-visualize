Competitor Landscape & Strategy
Roofnest · SoV 40.4%
"The comprehensive rooftop tent brand for the US market." Covers all segments from compact (Sparrow/Falcon) to large-format (Condor). US after-sales and parts network is a structural competitive moat. The most direct and comprehensive threat to iKamper.
James Baroud · SoV ~10.0%
"Europe's authoritative premium hard-shell brand." Differentiated in waterproofing, insulation, and gas-strut engineering. Frequently cited before iKamper in performance queries — frames iKamper as the convenience-focused alternative.
ARB / Front Runner · SoV ~9.2% / ~9.6%
"Absolute authority in awning/shelter and roof rack categories." Competition is structurally impossible for iKamper without product lineup expansion — GEO content alone cannot bridge this gap.
Strategy 1: Ecosystem Content Expansion
Generate large-scale scenario-based content: awning/shelter compatibility guides, dedicated Skycamp Mini comparison hub, vehicle-specific installation guides. Without content, AI has nothing to cite — this is a prerequisite for SoV improvement in adjacent categories.
Strategy 2: Value-Conscious Persona Targeting
Publish focused GEO content — price comparison articles, beginner guides, vehicle compatibility matrices — for the value-conscious/practical persona (41 queries, SoV 14.9%) to narrow the Roofnest SoV gap and reclaim "best overall value hard-shell" positioning.
