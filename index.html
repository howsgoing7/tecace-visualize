06 — Key Insights

Findings & Conclusions

Finding 01
New generation ≠ across-the-board gains
S26 does not universally outperform the S25. S25 leads on latency for both models.
Finding 02
S26's real strength is input processing
S26 shows a generational leap in Prefill TPS and TTFT — ideal for RAG and document Q&A pipelines.
Core Conclusion
The S26 is not a "faster S25." It is faster at understanding prompts, slower at generating output. Match device and model to the use case.
